The Process

How Speedshift Works

Our lab-to-market approach fast-tracks commercialization by bundling high-impact, complementary solutions — turning research and patented IP into market-ready products through a structured, demand-driven model.

01
Identify & Acquire IP
We partner with universities, government labs, and corporate R&D teams to license or co-develop high-potential patented technologies across our target sectors.
02
Develop & Test
Using our HiLab platform — combining digital twin simulation, hardware-in-the-loop testing, and robotic test automation — we rapidly advance prototypes using AI/ML and specialist partners.
03
Commercialize & Scale
Backed by our VC fund and ecosystem partnerships, we launch startups, secure government contracts, and build integrated platform solutions for enterprise and government customers.

Why Speedshift

What Makes Us Different

Most accelerators pick winners and mentor them. Speedshift does something different — we actively build the infrastructure, integrations, and ecosystem that de-risk commercialization for every partner.

✓
Demand-Driven, Not Pitch-Driven
We align technology with real corporate and government demand before investing — matching solutions to validated market needs rather than speculating on future adoption.
✓
Shared Infrastructure Reduces Risk
Startups access world-class testing equipment, digital twin simulation, and development resources without bearing the full upfront cost — accelerating timelines and preserving runway.
✓
Platform Integration Creates Larger Deals
We identify integration opportunities between portfolio companies, bundling complementary solutions into unified offerings that command larger government and enterprise contracts.
✓
Veteran Ownership Unlocks Government Access
Veteran-owned, AI-enabled service partners within the Speedshift ecosystem accelerate access to AFWERX, DoD, and other mission-critical government contract opportunities.
✓
Lab-to-Market with a Focus on Quick Exits
A mature leadership team with deep industry networks drives faster commercialization cycles and positions portfolio companies for strategic acquisitions or scale financing rounds.
