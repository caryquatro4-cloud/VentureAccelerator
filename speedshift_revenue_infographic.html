Revenue Model
How Speedshift generates returns across the full venture lifecycle
Core Thesis

Speedshift Ventures builds and commercializes emerging IP across dual-use technology and sports. Our four-stream revenue architecture generates near-term cash flow from services and long-term upside from equity and fund returns — reducing single-point-of-failure risk while compounding value across every relationship.

Revenue Architecture
1
Stream One
Direct Start-Up Agreements
Each portfolio company executes a commercial agreement for accelerator services, PDaaS, TaaS, and commercialization support.
Equity: 3%–8% stake, milestone-vested over 24–36 months
Cash retainer: $5K–$25K/month per company
Blended: Reduced fee + higher equity for early-stage ventures
Preferred partner discounts for SI, Digital Twin, and Dev roles ↓
Est. per cohort (10 cos)
$600K–$3M / yr
2
Stream Two
Capital Raise Participation
When Speedshift drives a portfolio company's fundraise — pitch prep, investor introductions, diligence, term sheets — it earns a participation fee.
Cash success fee: 2%–5% of total capital raised at close
Equity warrant: 0.5%–2% issued at round valuation in lieu of cash
Blended: 1%–2% cash + 0.25%–0.5% warrant
Speedshift retains pro-rata rights in subsequent rounds
Illustrative: $5M raise
$100K–$250K
3
Stream Three
Venture Fund & SPVs
Investors co-invest into Speedshift's portfolio through a managed fund or deal-by-deal Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs).
Micro Fund ($10M–$50M): 2%/yr mgmt fee + 20% carried interest above 8% hurdle
Deal SPV: $15K–$30K setup fee + 1%–2% mgmt fee + 15%–20% carry per deal
Direct co-investment: 1%–2% facilitation fee; board observer rights available
SPV can be launched within weeks — faster path to capital than a full fund
$25M fund → annual mgmt
~$500K + carry
4
Stream Four
Investor Tech Platform
Corporations, investors, or government entities that own proprietary IP engage Speedshift to accelerate, test, and commercialize their technologies.
Advisory + GTM: $50K–$200K retainer; 3%–5% success fee on first contract
PDaaS: $25K–$500K per development phase; IP co-ownership option
TaaS (HiLab): $5K–$15K/week; $60K–$120K annual membership
White-label sponsorship: $250K–$1M+/yr; exclusive vertical track + ROFR rights
Revenue share on contracts
5%–10% Year 1
⚡ Stream 1 Preferred Partner Discount Structure
Companies that designate Speedshift in a preferred technology role receive discounted service rates — converting discounts into high-margin integration, development, and recurring technology contracts
System Integrator of Choice
15–25% discount on fees
Discount differential may convert to additional equity at next financing round. Speedshift earns recurring SI contract revenue on every customer deployment.
Digital Twin Development Partner
10–20% discount on fees
IP co-ownership rights on jointly developed digital twin assets. Recurring SaaS/platform license revenue. Cross-portfolio reuse of built environments.
Web & Software Dev Partner
10–15% discount on fees
Source code ownership or licensing negotiated at agreement. Ongoing maintenance retainer. Platform stickiness increases long-term company dependency on Speedshift.
